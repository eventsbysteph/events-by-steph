About

Hi, I'm Steph!

I fell in love with weddings the same way a lot of people do — by planning my own. What hooked me wasn't any single task, it was the whole arc of it: starting out with no idea what actually goes into a wedding, and watching it all come together exactly right on the day itself. I want that process to feel exciting for the couples I work with, not just something to survive.

My job is to take the picture you already have in your head and help make sure it actually happens, so when you look back, your day feels like it truly reflected the two of you. I'm there to guide you through the parts that get overwhelming, and to build enough trust along the way that by the time your wedding day comes, what's in your head is in mine too.

These days, I'm especially drawn to smaller, intimate celebrations — backyard weddings, elopements, and micro weddings. That's where my heart is, but I'm happy to coordinate a wedding of any size.
