About Me

Hi, I'm Steph!

I've always been the person who notices the little things. The details others might overlook, the moments happening in the background, and the small touches that make people feel cared for. I love a good plan, a well-organized checklist, and knowing that everything has a place.

But more than anything, I genuinely love helping people.

There is something incredibly rewarding about taking the stress off a couple's shoulders and giving them the freedom to fully enjoy one of the happiest days of their lives. I love being the person who can step in, keep things calm, and make sure couples feel taken care of from start to finish.

And yes, I love weddings so much that there is a very good chance I'll tear up during your ceremony. Fair warning.

When I'm not coordinating weddings, you'll probably find me spending time with my husband and our two dogs, getting lost in a good book, working on a home project, or dreaming up my next creative idea.

I believe your wedding day should feel like you. It should be filled with the people you love, the moments you'll remember, and the freedom to actually experience it all.

I'd be honored to help make that happen.
