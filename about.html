About

Hi, I'm Steph!

I believe your wedding day should be filled with happy tears, laughter, and unforgettable moments. Not stress about timelines, vendors, or who's setting out the place cards.

My passion is bringing your vision to life while making the planning process feel organized, enjoyable, and effortless. Whether you need someone to keep everything running smoothly on your wedding day or a coordinator to help tie all the details together in the weeks leading up to "I do," I'm here to make sure every piece falls into place.

I'm known for staying calm under pressure, paying attention to the little details, and always having a backup plan. From managing your timeline and coordinating with vendors to solving last-minute surprises behind the scenes, my goal is for you, your family, and your friends to simply enjoy the celebration.

Every couple has a unique story, and I love creating an experience that reflects who you are. Your wedding isn't just another event to me. It's one of the biggest days of your life, and it's an honor to be a part of it.

When the big day arrives, you deserve to be fully present, soaking in every moment while someone you trust handles the logistics. That's exactly what I'm here for.

I genuinely love helping people, and there's something incredibly rewarding about taking the stress off a couple's shoulders so they can fully enjoy one of the happiest days of their lives. (And yes, I love weddings so much that I will absolutely tear up during your ceremony. Fair warning.)

I can't wait to celebrate with you and help make your wedding day everything you've dreamed of.
